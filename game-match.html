Game Match

Game Match is a mobile app that helps users discover personalized video game recommendations through a swipe-based interface and real-time AI matching.

Overview

GameMatch is an app that recommends games to users based on their preferences. Users can input their gaming preferences, and through the help of AI, the app searches through a list of games and matches them accordingly. The app uses various APIs to gather game information, such as platform, price, and genre.

Role: UX & UI Designer

Impact

GameMatch is an app that recommends games to users based on their preferences. Users can input their gaming preferences, and through the help of AI, the app searches through a list of games and matches them accordingly. The app uses various APIs to gather game information, such as platform, price, and genre.

The Problem: Game Discovery Overload

With thousands of games released across platforms each year, players often feel lost trying to find titles that match their taste and budget. Store algorithms, review videos, and endless scrolling are time-consuming and often fail to connect people with games they'll truly enjoy

Takeaway & Reflection

Game Match highlights how user-centered design and intelligent filtering can solve real problems in an oversaturated market—especially as platforms like Steam release tens of thousands of games each year. Through building it, I learned how to turn complexity into simplicity by combining real-time data, personalization, and intuitive UX. This project not only sharpened my technical skills in scalable app development, but also deepened my understanding of how thoughtful design can reduce friction, save users time, and deliver meaningful value. I'm especially grateful for the collaborative spirit of my team—each member brought unique strengths that made this project a success. Working together made the experience both productive and unforgettable.
